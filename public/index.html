About Us


Blueprint Analytics works with you to develop and execute analysis plans suited to your goals.

We specialize in research design, data analysis, data presentation and reporting, and implementing open data workflows.



Services


Research Design

We will assist you in developing a blueprint for your research. We will advise you on defining a clear and answerable research question, and on the choice of study type, data collection methodologies, and statistical analyses for your research.

Data Analysis

We will analyse your data from start to finish. We clean the data, generate graphical and numeric summaries, and undertake statistical inference testing. All analysis are presented in a comprehensive report that includes interpretations of the statistics and how to report the results.

Data presentation and reporting

We have solutions for effectively presenting your data, whether you need a table or a choropleth map. We customise the style, format, and functionality of the our reports and standalone plots and tables according to your needs. Do you need black-and-white versions for a print journal and full-colour versions for a presentation? Do you want a report with interactive or animated plots for your website? We have you covered.

Open data workflows

We specialise in developing workflows for reproducible research and open data using open-source software and platforms.
